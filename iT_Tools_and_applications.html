IT Tools And Applications

This course introduces the fundamentals of computers and their applications. It covers word processing, spreadsheets, presentations, databases, and internet tools. Students learn to use IT tools effectively for academic examinations and professional tasks.

Requirements
  • Basic knowledge of using a computer.
  • Interest in learning information technology tools and applications
  • Familiarity with operating systems like Windows or Linux (helpful but not mandatory)
  • Willingness to practice using office applications such as Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.
Who Can Take This Course?

This course is designed for students who wish to build a strong foundation in computer concepts and IT applications. It is ideal for beginners with little or no prior experience in information technology. The course is especially useful for students pursuing academic programs such as BCA, BBA, or other computer-related fields. .

IT Tools And Applications

This course introduces the basic concepts of computers and their practical uses. It covers hardware, software, operating systems, word processing, spreadsheets, presentations, databases, internet applications, and IT security. Students gain hands-on experience using MS Office tools for academic and professional tasks.

  • Unit 1: Computer Fundamentals
    Watch Lecture

    This unit explains the basic characteristics and components of a computer system, including input, output, storage units, and the CPU. It introduces the binary number system, conversions between binary and decimal, and coding systems like BCD and ASCII. The unit also highlights the applications of information technology in various fields such as education, business, and healthcare.
